What Cybersafe does

We catch the scams, so you don't have to.

Cybersafe watches the two most common phishing paths on iPhone, texts and websites, and gives you a private history of everything it flags.

Scam message reporting

Flag a suspicious text in one tap. Cybersafe saves the sender, timestamp, and the phrases that triggered the flag, so nothing gets lost after you swipe away.

Phishing website detection

Links are checked against known and emerging phishing domains before the page ever loads, so a bad link gets flagged, not clicked.

Deepfake site detection

Cloned login pages and deepfake video used to impersonate real brands or people are flagged before you're asked to hand anything over.

Centralized report database

Every scam Cybersafe flags, text or website, lands in one searchable history, kept privately on your phone.
